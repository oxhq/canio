# Code Of Conduct

## Our Standard

We expect contributors to keep discussion technical, respectful, and useful.

Examples of acceptable behavior:

- discussing tradeoffs directly and concretely
- criticizing ideas without attacking people
- helping keep claims, docs, and code aligned with reality

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment or personal attacks
- bad-faith review behavior
- intentionally misleading claims about product capabilities

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, issues, pull requests, or contributors whose behavior makes collaboration unsafe or unproductive.

For conduct concerns, contact `garaekz@gmail.com`.
